3.2 Einfacher Gleichstromkreis

Gegeben:
\( U_0 = 10V \\ P_2 = 1W, P_3 = 2W ~bei ~U_N = 12V \\ R_1 = 1 \Omega \\ \)

\( P_2 = 1W = 12V \cdot I_2 \Leftrightarrow I_2 = \frac{1}{12} A \\ P_3 = 2W = 12V \cdot I_3 \Leftrightarrow I_3 = \frac{1}{6} A \\ \\ \\ R_2 = \frac{P_2}{(I_2)²} , ~da ~P_2 = R \cdot I² \\ ~= \frac{1W}{(\frac{1}{12})²} = 144 \Omega \\ \\ \\ R_3 = \frac{2W}{(\frac{1}{6})²} ~ 72 \Omega \\ \\ \\ R_{ges} = R_1 + (R_2 || R_3) = R_1 + \frac{R_2 \cdot R_3}{R_2 + R_3} \\ ~= 1 \Omega + \frac{144 \Omega \cdot 72 \Omega}{144 \Omega + 72 \Omega} = 49 \Omega \\ \)

\( U_i = \frac{R_i}{R_{ges}} \cdot U_{ges} \\ U_1 = \frac{R_1}{R_{ges}} \cdot U_0 \\ ~= \frac{1 \Omega}{49 \Omega} \cdot 10V \approx 0,020408V \)
